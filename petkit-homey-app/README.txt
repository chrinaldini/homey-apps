Connect your PetKit smart pet devices to Homey. Supports litter boxes (Pura X, Pura MAX, Pura MAX 2), automatic feeders (Feeder Mini, Fresh Element series, YumShare), and water fountains (Eversweet series). Log in with your PetKit account during setup and all your devices will be added automatically.

Monitor litter box status, food and water levels, waste bin fill level, cat weight, and battery. Use Flow to automate cleaning cycles, get notified when the waste bin is full or food is running low, and dispense food on a schedule or triggered by other devices in your home.
